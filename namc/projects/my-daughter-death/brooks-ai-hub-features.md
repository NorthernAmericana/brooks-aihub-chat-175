## Overview

Purchasers of _My Daughter, Death: Frostbitten_ (Novel) or _My Daughter, Death_ (Video Game) will receive a unique access code that unlocks exclusive Brooks AI HUB features designed to enhance and deepen their experience with the story.

These features represent a new generation of interactive media analysis tools that sync with your progress and provide AI-powered insights into the rich lore and narrative of the My Daughter, Death universe.

## Core Features

### 1. Spoiler-Aware AI Analysis
- **Progress-Based Responses:** AI knows exactly where you are in the story (novel or game)
- **Safe Exploration:** Ask any question about lore, characters, or plot without fear of spoilers
- **Deep Dive Mode:** Unlock full spoiler analysis once you've completed sections
- **Context Understanding:** AI understands both novel and game continuity

### 2. Screenshot & Passage Analysis
- **Novel Screenshot Support:** Capture pages or passages from the novel
- **Game Screenshot Analysis:** Take in-game screenshots for AI breakdown
- **Lore Connections:** AI identifies how scenes connect to broader universe mythology
- **Character Insights:** Get deep analysis of character motivations and relationships
- **Symbolism Detection:** Uncover hidden themes and recurring motifs

### 3. Cloud Progress Tracking
- **Game Save Sync:** Early release game saves automatically backed up to cloud
- **Reading Progress:** Track where you are in the novel (compatible e-reader apps to be announced)
- **Cross-Media Progress:** See how your progress in one medium relates to the other
- **Progress Dashboard:** Visual timeline of your journey through both experiences
- **Achievement Tracking:** Milestones in story discovery and lore exploration

### 4. AI Lore Assistant
- **On-Demand Explanations:** Ask questions about New Bethlehem, The Watcher, or any story element
- **Character Database:** Deep profiles of all major and minor characters
- **Location Guide:** Detailed breakdowns of story locations and their significance
- **Timeline Navigation:** Understand the complex temporal structure of the narrative
- **Mythology Deep Dives:** Explore the cosmic mythology underpinning the story

### 5. Spoiler-Full Analysis Mode
- **Post-Completion Feature:** Available after finishing novel or game
- **Full Story Breakdown:** Complete analysis without restrictions
- **Thematic Essays:** AI-generated deep dives into major themes
- **Alternative Interpretations:** Explore different readings of ambiguous scenes
- **Canon Confirmation:** Verify your theories against established lore

## How It Works

1. **Purchase the Novel or Game:** Available early 2027
2. **Receive Your Access Code:** Unique code included with purchase
3. **Redeem in Brooks AI HUB:** Enter code to unlock features
4. **Sync Your Progress:** Connect your reading app or game account
5. **Start Exploring:** Ask questions, analyze scenes, and dive deep into lore

## Technical Details

- **Progress Detection:** Automatic tracking via cloud saves and reading apps
- **Spoiler Boundaries:** AI maintains strict spoiler-free zones based on progress
- **Multi-Format Support:** Works with novel (digital & physical via manual progress updates) and game
- **Real-Time Updates:** As you play/read, AI context updates in real-time
- **Privacy First:** Your progress and questions remain private
