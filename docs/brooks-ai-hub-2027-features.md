## The NPCAI Game Feature

### What is NPCAI?

NPCAI (Non-Player Character Artificial Intelligence) is revolutionary technology featured in _My Daughter, Death_ (Video Game):

- **Up to 60 Characters:** Each with unique LLM-powered personalities
- **Dynamic Conversations:** No scripted dialogue trees; natural interactions
- **Memory System:** NPCs remember your choices and previous conversations
- **Emotional Intelligence:** Characters respond authentically to your actions
- **Story Integration:** NPCs understand their role in the larger narrative

### How NPCAI Works with Brooks AI HUB

Brooks AI HUB enhances the NPCAI experience:

1. **Character Database:** Learn about NPCs you've met outside the game
2. **Conversation History:** Review past NPC interactions
3. **Relationship Maps:** Visualize how NPCs feel about you
4. **Personality Insights:** Understand NPC motivations and traits
5. **Dialogue Analysis:** AI explains subtext in NPC conversations

### Why NPCAI Matters

Traditional video game NPCs follow scripts. NPCAI characters are powered by language models that:

- Generate unique responses based on context
- Create emergent storytelling moments
- Remember and reference past interactions
- Form genuine-feeling relationships with the player
- Make the world feel alive and responsive

The result: _My Daughter, Death_ offers a level of character interaction never before possible in video games.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to buy both the novel and game to get features?**
A: No. Each purchase includes its own access code with full features for that medium. Buying both gives you features for both, but each stands alone.

**Q: Will my game progress work if I play offline?**
A: Yes. Offline play is supported. Progress syncs to cloud when you reconnect. Some NPCAI features may be limited offline.

**Q: Can I use these features for other books/games?**
A: Initially, these features are exclusive to _My Daughter, Death_ novel and game. More supported media will be announced as partnerships are established.

**Q: Is there a monthly fee for Brooks AI HUB features?**
A: No. Your access code from purchasing the novel or game provides lifetime access to these features for this project.

**Q: What if I don't want AI tracking my progress?**
A: All features are optional. You can use the novel or game completely standalone without connecting to Brooks AI HUB.

**Q: Will beta progress carry over to full release?**
A: Yes. Early Beta game progress and cloud saves will seamlessly transition to the full release.

**Q: How does spoiler prevention actually work?**
A: The AI tracks your progress (chapter/scene/game checkpoint) and maintains strict boundaries. It won't reference events, character revelations, or plot points beyond your current position.

**Q: Can I share my access code with friends?**
A: Access codes are intended for individual use. Sharing may result in deactivation. Each purchase includes one code.
