# Team & Ownership

## Overview

Brooks AI HUB is developed under **Northern Americana Tech (NAT)** and acts as a privacy-first control center OS for AI-powered mini-apps (“ATOs”), media experiences, and interactive systems.

This document defines:

- leadership structure
- decision ownership
- contributor pathways
- partnership entry points

Governance Model:

Founder-led with structured open collaboration.

The intent is to provide clarity and trust for early adopters, founders, and contributors.
